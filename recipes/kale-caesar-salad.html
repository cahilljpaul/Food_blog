Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

Total time: 45 minutes Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce. This nutritious salad is perfect for meal prep or a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kale, chopped
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 in middle.

    Time: 40 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa, chicken, kale, and Caesar dressing until coated.

    Time: 4 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish.

    Time: 1 minute

Nutrition Information

  • Calories: 450 per serving
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 25g

Tips & Notes

  • For a vegetarian version, replace chicken with grilled tofu or chickpeas
  • Make ahead: The salad keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 3 days
  • For extra flavor, try adding sun-dried tomatoes or roasted red peppers
